Subscribe to the Perl Weekly

In order to subscribe to the newsletter send an email to Gabor: gabor@szabgab.com with a subject: Subscribe to Perl Weekly.

I know that for most newsletters you just subscribe almost anonymously, but I'd like to know a bit about the readers of the newsletter.

Please include your name and where did you read about the Perl Weekly? What made you subscribe now?
I would also love to hear about you: How long have you been using Perl? What do you do with Perl? Where do you work?
If you have a website or a blog, please also include a link to it. If you use LinkedIn, please include a link to your profile.
