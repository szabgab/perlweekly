Perl Weekly - sponsors

 

The Perl Weekly newsletter reaches more than 5,000 Perl programmers every week. Not only that, but these are exactly those people who are interested in Perl a lot more than the average "Perl user". They put in time and energy following what's new in the Perl world.

If you are looking for Perl programmers this is probably the best place to reach them. You can put your job-ad in the newsletter and it will also remain in the archive. (See issue for example issue #83. )

Technical details:

  • A sponsored job ad can have a linked title and 2-3 lines of text.
  • The title is up to 80 characters.
  • The text is up to 300 characters.
  • The title will be linked to the URL of your choice.
  • It will be included in the newsletter and kept in the archive.
